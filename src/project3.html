Listen

I have created a website for project Listen. Listen is a collaboration between Interart and the artist known as Camilla Akraka. The purpose behind the work is to highlight art which takes a stand on injustice. The objective behind the website is to present the project and the idea it represents. This is something that i am personally passionate about, and I hope you are as well - help make a difference and visit the website.
